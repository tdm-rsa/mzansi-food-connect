## 8Ô∏è‚É£ GETTING PAID

### How Payments Work:

**For You (Vendor):**
1. Customer pays on your website
2. Money goes through Yoco (secure payment processor)
3. Yoco deposits money to YOUR bank account
4. You keep 100% of the sale price
5. Yoco charges small transaction fee (standard rates)

**Timeline:**
- Card payment processed instantly
- Money in your bank: 2-3 business days (Yoco standard)

**What You Pay:**
- Mzansi Food Connect: R159 or R215/month (subscription)
- Yoco: ~2.95% per transaction (industry standard)
- You: Keep the rest!

**Example:**
- Customer orders R100
- Yoco fee: ~R2.95
- You receive: ~R97.05
- Your subscription: R159/month (unlimited orders!)
